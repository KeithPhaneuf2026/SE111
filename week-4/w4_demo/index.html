About the movie Empire Records (1995)

A brief synopsis of the movie Empire Records: Twenty-four hours in the lives of the young employees at Empire Records when they all grow up and become young adults thanks to each other and the manager. They all face the store joining a chain store with strict rules.


DirectorAllan Moyle

WriterCarol Heikkenen


Production Company:Paramount

Release Date:April 1995

Genres:Comedy; Coming of Age

Rating:PG-13

Running Time:90 minutes
